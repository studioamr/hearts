QUESTIONS

What everyone asks

Is it free?

Yes. Jump in, we gift you your first fighter and 25 hearts. You play right in the browser — nothing to download.

What happens when I die?

You drop a heart on the floor (anyone can grab it) and you're out until the next round. Hit 0 hearts and you're out of the tournament. The last one with hearts banks everything they carry.

Can I really sell my animals?

Yes, in-game: every animal has a unique serial number and you can resell it in the marketplace for hearts.

When are the real prizes?

We're building the prize-pool tournaments. Join the beta on WhatsApp and we'll tell you first.

Is it like TowerFall?

It's a direct homage: limited arrows you grab off the floor, catching arrows by ducking, stacking chest powers… plus the heart battle royale you won't find anywhere else.
